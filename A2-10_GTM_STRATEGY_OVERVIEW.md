## GTM Pillars

### Pillar 1: Inbound Marketing (Content + Community)
Build awareness through valuable content where SREs hang out.

**Tactics**:
- Blog posts on incident response best practices
- Benchmarking reports on MTTR/escalation rates
- Podcast sponsorships (DevOps/SRE focused)
- Twitter/LinkedIn thought leadership
- Reddit engagement (/r/sre community)
- Incident response guide (free download)

**Outcome**: 500+ inbound leads by Month 3

---

### Pillar 2: Direct Sales (Enterprise)
Sales team targeting specific companies with high incident volume.

**Tactics**:
- Identify prospects: 100+ companies with incident frequency data
- Warm outreach: Founder + Sales team
- 30-min demos: Live incident diagnosis
- POC trial: Free Growth tier for 3 weeks
- Close: $5K-50K annual contracts

**Outcome**: 10-20 enterprise customers by Month 6

---

### Pillar 3: Community & Partnerships
Build trust through communities and partner endorsements.

**Tactics**:
- SRE community participation (The SRE Community, USENIX)
- Partnership with observability vendors (Datadog, New Relic)
- Integration with incident management platforms
- Sponsorship of incident response training
- Open source incident analysis tools

**Outcome**: 50+ partnership referrals by Month 6

---

### Pillar 4: Product-Led Growth
Let product speak for itself through free trial and word-of-mouth.

**Tactics**:
- 14-day free trial (Growth tier, all features)
- Viral loop: Share diagnosis results with team
- NPS tracking: Identify advocates
- Referral program: $500 credit per referred customer
- In-product education: Tooltips, guides, best practices

**Outcome**: 20% of customers from referrals by Month 6

## Sales Process

### Sales Cycle for SRE Teams (1-2 weeks)
1. **Day 1**: Customer discovers Claude Debug Copilot (inbound/referral)
2. **Day 1-2**: Visits landing page, reads value prop
3. **Day 2-3**: Signs up for free trial
4. **Day 3-7**: Uses product, experiences value
5. **Day 7-10**: Purchases Growth tier ($499/month)
6. **Total**: 7-10 day sales cycle

### Sales Cycle for Enterprise (4-8 weeks)
1. **Week 1**: Sales outreach (email, LinkedIn)
2. **Week 1-2**: Discovery call (30 min, understand pain)
3. **Week 2**: Product demo (60 min, live incident)
4. **Week 2-3**: POC trial (14 days, Growth tier free)
5. **Week 3-4**: POC results review (metrics shown)
6. **Week 4-6**: Contract negotiation
7. **Week 6-8**: Signature, onboarding begins
8. **Total**: 6-8 week sales cycle
