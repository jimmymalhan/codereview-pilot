## TTS Optimization Notes

1. **Pacing:** Short declarative sentences for clean TTS delivery. No filler words.
2. **Numbers:** "ninety-two percent" spelled out for natural TTS pronunciation.
3. **Pauses:** Sentence boundaries at each scene transition create natural breathing pauses.
4. **Emphasis words:** "seconds," "one click," "every assumption," "evidence first" carry the key message.
5. **Avoid:** No jargon, no URLs spoken aloud, no acronyms that TTS might mispronounce.
